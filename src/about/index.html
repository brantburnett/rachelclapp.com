About Me

 

Rachel Clapp brings an extensive television and media background to her voice over experience. She spent eight years as a producer with WCNC, the NBC affiliate in Charlotte, NC. At WCNC, she earned eight Emmy nominations for her producing work. She also won the prestigious National Headliner Award for her undercover investigative producing work. She’s also worked as a field producer with The Washington Bureau in Washington, DC and a show producer for The Slant, a college football show airing on Fox Sports Net. Her voice over experience ranges from television and radio commercials, to narration for television shows and internal corporate videos.
